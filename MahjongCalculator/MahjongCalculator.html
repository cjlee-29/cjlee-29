Category: Tools • Utility

About This App

3P Mahjong Calculator is an offline utility tool designed to simplify Mahjong scoring for 3-player games. Perfect for Mahjong players and tournaments — it helps you quickly calculate scores, adjust penalties, and keep track of total points after each round.

  • ⚙️ Easy-to-use calculator for 3-player Mahjong scoring
  • 📱 Offline support — no internet connection needed
  • 🧮 Automatically updates total scores after every round
  • 💾 Save or reset your match with one click
  • 🎨 Simple, clean UI optimized for mobile and desktop
